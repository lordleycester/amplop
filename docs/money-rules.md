# Amplop Money Rules

These are the core budgeting rules Amplop should keep true.

- Income increases Ready to Assign.
- New budget account balances increase Ready to Assign.
- Account balance adjustments count as inflow or outflow, so they affect Ready to Assign when they add or remove budget money.
- Assigning money to a category decreases Ready to Assign.
- Spending from a category decreases that category's Available amount.
- Transfers between accounts do not change total net worth.
- Credit card payments are transfers, not expenses.
- Installments create monthly obligations only for their active months.
