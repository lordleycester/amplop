# Onboarding Copy

This document tracks the editable text for the non-category parts of Amplop onboarding.

It does not cover the category starter survey questions. Those live in `docs/category-survey-plan.md`.

## Intro Modal

### Header Eyebrow

Quick start

### Header Title

Budget every rupiah

### Intro Explanation

Zero-based envelope budgeting means every rupiah gets a job before you spend it.

### Intro Supporting Text

That doesn't mean you spend everything. Investments, debt payoff, and emergency funds are jobs too.

## Explainer Cards

### Card 1

Title:

Start with real money

Body:

Add your bank, cash, and card accounts. Amplop treats money you already have as the pile you can give jobs to.

### Card 2

Title:

Fill the envelopes

Body:

Assign that money into categories like rent, groceries, savings, or subscriptions until Ready to Assign reaches zero.

### Card 3

Title:

Spend from a category

Body:

When you record an expense, choose the account it came from and the envelope it should reduce.

### Card 4

Title:

Adjust as life happens

Body:

If one envelope runs short, move money from another.

The setup coach appears after the category survey is saved.

### Step 1

Eyebrow:

Step 1 of 4

Title:

Set your targets

Body:

Targets are the plan: what rent, food, savings, subscriptions, and debt should need in a normal month.
